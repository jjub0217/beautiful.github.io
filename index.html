Her black evening dresses covered with sequins in the 1920s responded to the quest of graphic impact and the desire to shine," said Chanel.

Dubbed "Chanel Wonderland" the new space is a mesmerizing mega podium designed to create a magical experience for travelers during the holiday season. With its grand dimensions, standing at 7 meters in height and occupying approximately 130 square meters, this podium is set to redefine the duty-free experience. It is brought to life with the theme of "the magic of a special holiday with Chanel."
